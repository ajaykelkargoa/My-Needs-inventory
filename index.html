üìñ How to Use Your Needs Inventory

Throughout Your Day:

  • Notice when you feel energized, frustrated, or emotional
  • Browse the needs library to identify what's alive
  • Click to add needs to your personal inventory
  • Don't judge - just capture what's present

For Each Need:

  • Click "Deep Dive" to explore what the need means
  • Connect to body signals and emotions
  • Identify what would meet the need
  • Choose small steps to honor it

Weekly Review:

  • Which needs showed up most frequently?
  • What patterns do you notice in your inventory?
  • Which needs are you most/least comfortable with?
  • How might unmet needs be affecting your leadership?
