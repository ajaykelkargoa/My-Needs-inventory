What You Might Notice

Everyone's experience is different, but here's what some people observe when they track their needs consistently:

1-3

First Few Days

You might start noticing needs you hadn't consciously recognized before. This can feel surprising or relieving.

1

After One Week

Patterns may begin to emerge. You might see themes in your data that weren't obvious before.

2

After Two Weeks

Decision-making might feel clearer as you become more aware of what you're optimizing for.

3+

Ongoing Practice

Needs awareness could become a natural part of your reflection and decision-making process.

A gentle note: There's no "right" way to use this tool. Some people find daily tracking helpful, others prefer to use it when they're feeling stuck or curious. Trust your own rhythm and what feels sustainable for you.
